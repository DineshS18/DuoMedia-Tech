Vairamuthu ( born 13 July 1953 ) is a famous Tamil film lyricist and poet . He has received the Indian Government Award for Best Lyricist seven times. He first wrote the song "Ponmalai Padhu" in the movie Shadows (1980) and has written 5800 songs till January 2009. Earlier with Ilayaraja and later with A.R. The songs he rendered with Raghuman have won many accolades and accolades.. He was born in Vadugapatti near Periyakulam , Theni District , Tamil Nadu as the son of Ramasamy - Angammal in a farming family. Studied Tamil Literature at Pachaiyappan College, Chennai . In 1980, he first composed a song in the movie " Shadows ", which starts as Ekta Eru Ponmalai Poda . His wife's name is Ponmani. He has two sons Madan Karki and Kapilan
