Conjuring Kannappan is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language comedy horror film written and directed by Selvin Raj Xavier in his directorial debut and produced by AGS Entertainment. The film stars Sathish and Regina Cassandra, alongside Nassar, Anandaraj, Saranya Ponvannan, VTV Ganesh, and Redin Kingsley.The film was announced in September 2023. Principal photography had commenced and ended before the announcement. The film has music composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja, cinematography handled by S. Yuva and editing by Pradeep E. Ragav.Conjuring Kannappan released theatrically on 8 December 2023 and opened to positive reviews from critics.
