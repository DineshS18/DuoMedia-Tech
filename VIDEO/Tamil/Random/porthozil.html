Por Thozhil is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language thriller film directed by Vignesh Raja in his directorial debut, written by Alfred Prakash. The film stars R. Sarathkumar, Ashok Selvan and Nikhila Vimal, with Sarath Babu, Nizhalgal Ravi, Sunil Sukhada and Santhosh Keezhattoor in supporting roles. The music was composed by Jakes Bejoy, while the cinematography and editing were handled by Kalaiselvan Sivaji and Sreejith Sarang. Por Thozhil was released theatrically on 9 June 2023 to positive reviews from critics and became a commercial success at the box office.
