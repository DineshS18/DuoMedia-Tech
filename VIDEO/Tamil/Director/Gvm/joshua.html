Joshua Imai Pol Kaakha (translation - Joshua: Protect like an Eyelid) is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film written and directed by Gautham Vasudev Menon and produced by Ishari K. Ganesh. The film, the first of a planned shared universe, stars Varun in the title role, alongside Krishna and debutante Raahei. It revolves around a contract killer-turned bodyguard seeking to protect his client from a Mexican drug cartel.The film began production in late 2019 but concluded only in 2021-2022, following delays related to the COVID-19 pandemic. The music was composed by Karthik, while S. R. Kathir and Anthony handled the cinematography and editing of the film. Joshua: Imai Pol Kaakha was released in theatres on 1 March 2024 to mixed reviews from critics.
